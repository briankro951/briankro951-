Graphic Score 1 :: Left -> Right

Created from an exersice to compose modular music in graphic notation using TouchDesigner.

There are 4 sections. The idea is entropy. Starting from the left, create a sound/meoldy/harmony, using any instrument desired, inspired by the graphic.

Your sound may evolve throughout the performance, but keep the theme at each section consistent until you move on to the next section

Below is video render of the graphic score (no sound). Start anywhere from the video, and have fun. :)
