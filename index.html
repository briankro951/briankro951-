About Me!

I'm an aspiring composer hoping to making it into the film world as either a composer or sound designer, but hopfully primarily as musician.

My education in music started while I was in elementary, but I never recieved the resources to be properly prepared for more advanced education even by the time I graduated Highschool, so I mainly started my real education when I attended the University of California, Santa Barbara. There I sharpened my basic skills needed as a musician, and learned beyond. I played in the UCSB Percussion Ensemble all four years I attended there, learned to orchestrat for acoustic orchestral instruments, and learned to produce electronic music with digital-audio-work stations, as well as using hardware syntheresizer to use for my performances, solos and acompanied.

In the final year of my undergrade at UCSB, I was fortunate enough (and purely from coisidence) to have encoutnered a film crew in need of a sound operator. I was inexperienced, but determined to learn. I didn't even think of it as a gateway to the film world, I simply wanted to apply my production passion to the film world in some way. For the crew, and many more in the near future, I have helped with on field recording, as well as post production.
